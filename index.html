About James

Welcome to the internet home of James Nassar, the current play-by-play broadcaster for the Laredo Lemurs. The 2017 season will be his fifth season broadcasting in professional baseball, as well as his second season as the lead broadcaster in Laredo. On this website, you will be able to find some basic information about him and his broadcasting abilities, including his resume, play-by-play samples, writing examples, and more. So, look around and take a listen! Follow James on Twitter, SoundCloud, and LinkedIn and feel free to contact him with any questions or comments.
